If you have built a form online and you’re not a developer, we’d like to hear from you.

We’ve put together a quick survey that should take less than 5 minutes to complete. Please fill it out, and share it with anyone within the Government of Canada that might be interested in participating.

Why are we asking?

The Canadian Digital Service Platform Team is designing a way for government employees to build forms online without the help of development resources. We want to create a site that is not only safe and accessible but also one that is easy to use and meets the needs of government employees.

For this to be possible, we need to hear from anyone in government who has built a form online using a form builder such as Microsoft Forms, Google Forms, SurveyMonkey, WordPress, Drupal, or any of the multitudes of others like them.

Go to survey.
