Didn’t find what you were looking for? Want to apply to the Outreach stream? Tell us why you’re right for the job. We recognize that everyone brings skills and experiences to the table and that nobody “checks all the boxes”.

Outreach is right for you if:

  • You have experience in building an impressive public communications presence with a background in one or more of the following: developing and implementing communications strategies, content writing, stakeholder engagement, and training and learning.
  • You're ideally fluent in both English and French.
  • You are a communicator who knows how to write in different mediums and channels, like video, social media, and graphic design.
  • You readily learn about new areas, and through your communication, help others learn and engage with new ideas.
