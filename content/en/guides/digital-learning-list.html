CDS is often asked to suggest materials on digital government. The resources below are a helpful introduction to digital service delivery and technology modernization. The list also covers digital’s impact on marginalized or under-represented groups.

Several newsletters are also great resources: the Apolitical Digital Government Atlas, the Brookfield Institute, Code for Canada, The Commons, the GC Data community, GC Learning, and Public Digital. For Government of Canada-specific materials, read Canada’s Digital Ambition or explore the Canadian School for Public Service’s Discover Series.
