Sign up for future website testing

We're conducting research to improve the CDS website. This research will help us make the site easier to understand and use.

We're looking to speak with people who are:

  • Federal public servants working in service delivery

If that's you, or someone you know, please contact Victoria Chan at victoria.chan@tbs-sct.gc.ca to indicate interest in participating in future testing. Once our team is ready to begin testing, we will contact you with details and answer any questions you have.

How it will work:

  • Research sessions will be conducted virtually using Microsoft Teams or Google Meets.
  • We will ask you to share your screen as you navigate the website and talk us through your experience with it.
  • Sessions will be around 60 minutes and take place during the working hours of 9 am to 4 pm ET.

Participation and privacy

  • Signing up to participate gives us permission to contact you about research opportunities. You are under no obligation to participate.
  • Participation is voluntary and participants may stop at any time for any reason.
  • Responses will be confidential, which means they will not be linked back to anyone.
  • Your participation and answers will not impact your relationship with CDS or the Government of Canada.
  • CDS handles personal information in accordance with the Privacy Act. We will share our Privacy Statement before the session begins to let you know how the information will be used.
