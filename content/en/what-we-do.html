The Canadian Digital Service is always on the lookout for new government partnerships.

We work together

Assembling teams made up of both CDS employees and your departmental staff, we pair delivery expertise with program and business knowledge.

We grow together

We learn by doing together. By adopting iterative and people-centred methods, we can find solutions that work. . When our partnership ends, you’ll be empowered to support and continuously improve your service.

Partnerships

Reach

We want to take on service problems that make a real impact. This could mean reaching a lot of people in small ways, or a small group of people in big ways. The numbers don’t necessarily matter; the impact does.

Readiness

We don’t own the service; you do. We believe products are most successful if:

  • you’re keen to find a solution to the problem together through research,
  • your organization has dedicated staff who are eager to work through the problem,
  • you’re excited to work iteratively and learn to use people-centred methods, and
  • you’re committed to working in the open, sharing our successes and failures publicly.

Replicability

We consider whether the problem you face is common across government. If it is, chances are another government department can use the solution too.

Interested?

You can contact us at cds-snc@tbs-sct.gc.ca. We’ll set up a chat to learn about your service problem and answer any questions you have about CDS.
