The Canadian Digital Service is always on the lookout for new government partnerships. We work with federal departments and agencies across Canada to deliver simple and reliable services.

We work together

Assembling teams made up of both CDS employees and your departmental staff, we pair delivery expertise with program and business knowledge. As a team, let’s find solutions to the problem through research and testing.

We grow together

Working iteratively using people-centred methods can be tough to navigate. We’ll work closely together so that when our partnership ends, you’ll be empowered to support and continuously improve your service.

How we prioritize partnerships

Reach

We want to take on service problems that make a real impact. This could mean reaching a lot of people in small ways, or a small group of people in big ways. The numbers don’t necessarily matter; the impact does.

Readiness

We don’t own the service; you do. Here’s what it means to be ready:

  • you’re keen to find a solution to the problem together through research,
  • your organization has dedicated staff who are eager to work through the problem,
  • you’re excited to work iteratively and learn to use people-centred methods, and
  • you’re committed to working in the open, sharing our successes and failures publicly.

Replicability

Though not as prioritized as reach and readiness, we also consider whether the problem you face is common across government. If it is, chances are another government department can use the solution too.

Interested?

You can contact us at cds-snc@tbs-sct.gc.ca. We’ll set up a chat to learn about your service problem and answer any questions you have about CDS.
