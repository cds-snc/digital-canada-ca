The Canadian Digital Service works in small teams embedded with partner departments to design and build simple and reliable services. Along the way, we work with our partners in iterative and people-centred methods. When our work is done, they’re empowered to support and continuously improve their service.

We’re here to help.

How we work

We act on what people tell us they need. Adding new technology to people’s lives doesn't solve problems. Putting people at the heart of technology does. Through design research, we involve people every step of the way to make sure what we build works for those who actually use it.

We continuously improve our services

People and technology change; our services should change with them. We develop digital services through continuous, incremental improvement.

We hack the bureaucracy

With the right tools, federal departments and agencies can do their best work. We interpret and inform policy to help scale digital methods across government. We help reduce risk for departments by being a safe place to try new ways of working.

We work in the open

Fork our code. It’s published openly on the web, along with how we work. We share our successes and failures to help others learn from what we do. We want to show what’s possible and empower government to make it a reality.
