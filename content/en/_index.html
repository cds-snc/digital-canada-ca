The Canadian Digital Service partners with government departments and agencies to design and build simple and reliable technology. We’re here to improve people’s lives by putting their needs and concerns at the centre of government services.

How we work

We put people first

We act on what people tell us they need. Adding new technology to people’s lives doesn't solve problems. Putting people at the heart of technology does. Through design research, we involve people every step of the way to make sure that what we build works for those who actually use it.

We continuously improve our products

People and technology change; our products should change with them. We develop digital services through continuous improvement. This means we test our work and tools, find ways to make them better, and test again.

We hack the bureaucracy

With the right tools and practices, federal departments and agencies can do their best work. We interpret and inform policy to help scale digital methods across government. We help reduce risk for departments by being a safe place to try new ways of working.

We work in the open

Checkout our code. It’s published openly on the web, along with how we work. We share our successes and failures to help others learn from what we do. We want to show what’s possible and empower government to make it a reality.
