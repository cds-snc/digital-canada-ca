Help us improve our service to send notifications to the public

Are you a government employee who has to send a lot of messages frequently to the public? Are you curious to discover new tools that could help you send information to Canadians?

If you or someone on your team is in charge of sending notifications or messages (e-mails, text messages) to your users, we would like to chat with you! This conversation will help us better understand your needs and improve the notification service we are working on.

If you would like to help us, or if you know someone who could help us, please contact antoine.garcia-suarez@tbs-sct.gc.ca to find out how to get involved.

How you can help

We would like to talk to you (by phone or video chat) and get involved in a few activities to receive your feedback. The discussion should not last more than 30 minutes, and it can take place at a time that suits you best.

Confidentiality of your information

Your participation is voluntary. So you can decide not to participate anytime, for any reason.

We (the Canadian Digital Service) will ensure that your answers are confidential, which means they will not identify you.

Your participation and your answers will not affect your relationship with the Canadian Digital Service or any other Government of Canada institution.

We treat your personal information in accordance with the Privacy Act. You will receive a copy of our privacy notice.
